LIMITLESS POSIBILITIES

ChillHub is a refrigerator with two seamlessly integrated USB hubs. This first of its kind platform enables development of new hardware products that benefit from operating inside a cooled space. From niche to mass-market, ChillHub will allow the developer community to create new uses for refrigerators.

SPECS

Specs:
- 8 USB ports in 2 different locations in the fridge
- USB power: 5V, 4A, 20W (wire dependent)
- WiFi connected (wireless n and earlier)
- Software developers kit (node.js)
- Open source iOS app
- ChillHub refrigerator is available in Stainless, White, Black, and Slate (link to shopify)
