WHAT IS CHILLHUB?

When the FirstBuild community started in May 2014, community members were submitting ideas for new refrigerator peripheral devices. The missing links were power and internet connectivity. Community members Brian Wagner and Nicolas Greniè designed the first-ever internet connected USB ports in a refrigerator. Chill Hub opened up the refrigerator to the development of internal devices that connect effortlessly to the "FirstBuild" iOS app.

WHAT DOES IT DO?

Chill Hub is a refrigerator with two seamlessly integrated USB hubs. This first-of-its-kind platform enables development of new hardware products that benefit from operating inside a cooled space. From niche to mass market, Chill Hub will allow the developer community to create new devices that could benefit all refrigerator users.

FEATURES

INTEGRATED

Provides seamless integration with the refrigerator. Allows access to sensor data and control of refrigerator components.

POWERFUL

Build-in USB power and WiFi connectivity provide common elements required for nearly any application.

OPEN

The development platform is open, allowing FirstBuild community members to share and improve upon applications.

MOBILE

The open-source, iOS-compatible app connects you to ChillHub anywhere.


DETAILED SPECS


  • 8 USB ports in 2 different locations in the fridge
  • USB power: 5V, 4A, 20W (wire dependent)
  • WiFi connected (wireless n and earlier)
  • Software developers kit (node.js)
  • Open source iOS app

ADDITIONAL FEATURES

  • Available in Stainless, White, Black, and Slate.
  • Dual icemaker - An icemaker in both compartments gives you more ice whenever you need it.
  • Full-width electronic temperature-controlled drawer with colored LED lights - 3 accurate settings deliver the optimal temperature for your favorite foods, while colored lights indicate the chosen setting.
  • TwinChill evaporators - Separate climates in the fresh food and freezer sections help keep foods fresh.
  • Showcase LED lighting - Positioned throughout the interior and under fresh food doors to spotlight foods inside the refrigerator and in the freezer.
  • Ice and water dispenser - Easily fills large containers like pitchers or ice buckets with filtered water and ice.
